Wedding Preparation To-Do List

Pre-Made To-Do List

6-12 Months Before the Wedding

Set a Budget

Determine overall budget and allocate funds for different aspects.

Create a Guest List

Draft a preliminary guest list for invitations.

Choose a Date and Venue

Select a wedding date and book the venue for the ceremony and reception.

Hire a Wedding Planner (if desired)

Consider hiring a planner to assist with logistics.

Select Wedding Theme/Style

Decide on a theme, colors, and overall style.

Start Researching Vendors

Look for caterers, photographers, florists, and entertainment options.

4-6 Months Before the Wedding

Book Vendors

Finalize contracts with key vendors (caterer, photographer, etc.).

Choose Attire

Shop for wedding dress/tuxedo and schedule fittings.

Plan the Ceremony

Decide on officiant and ceremony details (readings, music).

Send Save-the-Dates

Mail out save-the-date cards to guests.

2-4 Months Before the Wedding

Order Invitations

Design and order wedding invitations and other stationery.

Plan the Menu

Work with caterer to finalize the menu.

Arrange Transportation

Book transportation for the wedding day (limos, shuttles).

Plan Honeymoon

Research and book honeymoon destination and travel arrangements.

1-2 Months Before the Wedding

Send Invitations

Mail out wedding invitations to guests.

Finalize Details with Vendors

Confirm details and timelines with all vendors.

Purchase Wedding Rings

Shop for and purchase wedding bands.

Create a Wedding Day Timeline

Draft a schedule for the wedding day, including vendor arrival times.

1-2 Weeks Before the Wedding

Confirm RSVP

Follow up with guests who havenâ€™t responded.

Prepare Payments and Tips

Prepare payment envelopes for vendors to be distributed on the wedding day.

Pack for Honeymoon

Organize and pack items for the honeymoon trip.

Practice Vows

Review and practice vows if writing your own.

Final Days Before the Wedding

Final Dress Fitting

Have the final fitting for wedding attire.

Delegate Tasks

Assign tasks to trusted friends or family for the wedding day.

Relax and Pamper Yourself

Schedule time for relaxation or pampering (spa, manicure).

Wedding Day

Eat Breakfast

Remember to eat a good breakfast to keep your energy up.

Enjoy Your Day!

Take a moment to breathe and enjoy every moment of your special day.
